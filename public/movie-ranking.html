The Best Movies according to Derlo

My top 3 movies of all-time

Starship Troopers

"Starship Troopers" is often considered one of the best movies of all time for a few reasons. It's got amazing special effects and intense action scenes that keep you on the edge of your seat. But beyond that, it's actually a really smart movie. It uses all the over-the-top military stuff to make fun of and criticise things like fascism and propaganda. So, it's entertaining and makes you think at the same time. Plus, it has a unique style and memorable characters that stick with you long after you watch it. That's why so many people love it and keep talking about it.

Avatar

"Avatar" is a favorite movie for a lot of people because it has amazing visuals and a really engaging story. The special effects were groundbreaking at the time, and the 3D experience made it feel like you were actually on the alien planet of Pandora. The story is also really compelling, with themes about protecting the environment and respecting other cultures. Plus, the emotional connections between the characters, especially Jake and Neytiri, really draw you in. It was a huge hit when it came out and broke all kinds of box office records, making it a big deal in movie history. Overall, it's a movie that combines stunning visuals with a heartfelt story, which is why so many people love it.

Interstellar

"Interstellar" is considered one of the best movies of all time for a few key reasons. First off, the visuals are absolutely stunning. The way it shows space, black holes, and different planets is really breathtaking. The story is also incredibly moving and thought-provoking.
